Comics by Corin DaRosa

This mini-comic collects some of the things I've learned during my time as a union organizer at Kickstarter United in the hopes that it will help others to unionize their own workplaces.

Topics covered include how to begin the process of organizing your coworkers, what the practical steps are to unionizing, and things to expect from your boss along the way.
